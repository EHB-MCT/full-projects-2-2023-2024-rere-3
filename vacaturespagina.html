PHD

Description

This doctoral research is part of the clinical study 'ActiveAge at Home', funded by the FWO TBM programme. In this study, the current care of frail, community-dwelling elderly people will be compared with two forms of a functional exercise program.

Profil

• You have a Master of Science degree in a domain that is relevant to this project.

• You are intrinsically motivated to conduct research and more specifically within the field of the elderly and active aging.

• You work accurately.

• You are fluent in Dutch and English and have an academic writing style.
