Our Lab

The Brubotics Rehabilitation
Research Center is the new
state-of-the-art lab of the BruBotics
Rehabilitation Research group at VUB.
It serves as an interdisciplinary hub for human
movement analysis and technology-supported
rehabilitation research.

Vision

In recent decades,
rehabilitation technologies like robotics,
augmented and virtual reality,
and neuromodulation have rapidly increased.
As these become more accessible,
there is a growing need for expertise and
scientific evidence on their effectiveness
and impact on physical and mental well-being.
